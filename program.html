The Program

This highly personalised coaching process is designed to restore your health and support sustainable weight loss through a structured, step-by-step approach over 12 weeks.

This is not a workout program. The focus is on your eating habits and lifestyle. Each week, you will be given a clear set of tasks and guidelines on what to do and how to eat, allowing for steady progress through small, consistent wins. You will be held accountable through daily checklists, along with a follow-up session at the start of each week to review your progress, introduce the next steps, and make any necessary adjustments. You will be guided and supported throughout the process, with clear direction and accountability, so that you are never left guessing what to do next.

This is not another quick fix.
It is not passive.
And it is not effortless.

At the same time, you will not be thrown into overwhelming change. The program is designed to help you adapt one step at a time, making the process both achievable and sustainable.

As your body begins to function the way it should, things that once felt difficult — controlling appetite, maintaining energy, and losing weight — begin to feel simpler and more natural.

This is a serious approach for people who are ready to commit to lasting change.

  • A personalised nutrition and lifestyle strategy
  • Meal guidance and practical recipes
  • One-on-one coaching and support
  • Daily structure and accountability
  • Ongoing adjustments based on your progress
  • Personalised supplement guidance

  • All consultations throughout the 12-week program
